The Best Movies according to Drishti

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Martian

It depicts how to survive on a planet that's not known to us with unfavourable conditions for survival.

3 Idiots

This movie teaches you to follow your passion and not succumb to societal or parental pressures for your career choices.

Kal Ho Naa Ho

Heart-warming movie that gives us the lesson to not take life for granted and enjoy each and every moment of the present.
